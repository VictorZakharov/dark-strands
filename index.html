SURVIVAL GUIDE

SHIFT+? to close
⚔ Movement
Walk & Sprint
Use WASD to move freely. Hold SHIFT to sprint at double speed.
Jump & Double Jump
Press SPACE to jump. Press again mid-air for a double jump. You can also jump briefly after walking off a ledge.
Zoom
Hold Right-click for 3x zoom. Scout distant threats and landmarks before approaching.
💬 Interaction
Interact
Press E near doors, soldiers, flowers, rocks, torches, or beds. Watch for the [E] prompt floating in the world.
Throw & Place
Select a stone and left-click to throw. Press E with a stone selected to enter placement mode -- a green preview shows where it will land. Click to place.
Flowers & Torches
Pick up flowers and torches with E. Select and left-click to place them -- flowers go on terrain, torches on walls or ground.
🎯 Camera & HUD
Camera
Press V to switch between first-person and over-the-shoulder third-person view.
Hotbar
Press 1-5 to select slots. Hold ALT for a virtual cursor -- drag items between slots to reorder.
Pause
TAB pauses with mouse trapped. ESC frees cursor (game keeps running). Click to re-lock.
Fast-Forward
Hold Q to run the world at 3x speed -- time, movement, and animations all accelerate.
⚔ Touch Controls
Move
Touch and drag the left side of the screen. A virtual joystick appears where you touch.
Look Around
Swipe the right side of the screen to rotate the camera. Use both thumbs -- left to move, right to look.
Jump
Tap the JUMP button (bottom-right). Tap again mid-air for a double jump.
Interact
Hold still on the right side for 1 second near doors, soldiers, flowers, rocks, torches, or beds. A progress circle fills to confirm.
Use Item
Tap the USE button to throw stones, enter placement mode, or place torches/flowers.
Hotbar & Camera
Tap a hotbar slot to select it. Tap CAM to switch between first and third person.
🏠 Exploration
Buildings
Stone structures with doorways, breakable windows, and furniture. Some rise two stories with staircases, tables, chairs, and beds.
Rooftops
Climb onto rooftops if you can find a way up. Slanted roofs are slippery -- thrown stones will roll off. Flat roofs make good vantage points.
Terrain & Water
Rolling hills shape the landscape. Walk into water for an underwater view -- movement slows to 40% and gravity gets floaty.
Snow Biome
Toggle from the main menu for a frozen landscape. Water turns to walkable ice and trees take on a wintry look.
💥 Rocks & Physics
Throwing
Select a stone and left-click to throw. Stones bounce off walls and terrain with realistic physics. Slanted roofs are slippery -- stones roll right off.
Placement Mode
Press E with a stone selected to toggle placement mode. A green preview shows where the stone will land. Click to place it gently instead of throwing.
Rock Splitting
Thrown stones that hit stationary pebbles will knock them loose with momentum transfer. Chain reactions are possible -- experiment with angles.
Breaking Windows
Throw a stone at a window pane to shatter it. Glass shards scatter on impact. Broken windows let projectiles pass through freely.
💬 Creatures & NPCs
Soldiers
Green-tinted warriors wander the land. Get close to hear their stories -- some wise, some strange.
Foxes
Skittish foxes roam the wilderness. They flee if you get too close -- try sneaking up on them.
☀ Day & Night
Time Cycle
Enable from the main menu. The sun arcs across the sky, torches ignite at dusk, and stars emerge at night.
Sleeping
Find a bed inside a building and interact to sleep until morning. Requires the day/night cycle to be enabled.
🎯 HUD
Minimap
Top-right corner shows your position (yellow dot), buildings (brown), and flowers (cyan).
