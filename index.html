SURVIVAL GUIDE

SHIFT+? to close
⚔ Movement & Combat
Walk & Sprint
Use WASD to move freely. Hold SHIFT to sprint at double speed -- but beware, stamina is limited.
Jump
Press SPACE to leap over obstacles, scale boulders, and reach second-story staircases.
Zoom
Hold Right-click for 3x zoom. Scout distant threats and landmarks before approaching.
🏠 Exploration
Buildings
Discover stone structures with doorways, glass windows, and torchlit interiors. Some rise two stories with climbable staircases.
Terrain & Water
Rolling hills shape the landscape. Walk into water for an underwater view -- movement slows to 40% and gravity gets floaty.
Snow Biome
Toggle from the main menu for a frozen landscape. Water turns to walkable ice and trees take on a wintry look.
💬 Interaction
Doors
Approach a door and press E to swing it open or shut. Watch for the [E] prompt floating in the world.
Soldiers
Green-tinted warriors wander the land. Get close and press E to hear their stories -- some wise, some strange.
Flowers
Glowing blooms dot the terrain. Press E to collect them into your hotbar. Select the flower slot and left-click to enter placement mode -- a green preview shows where it will be planted.
Rocks & Stones
Small rocks can be picked up with E. Select the stone slot and left-click to throw them in an arc. Landed stones can be picked up again.
Torches
Press E near a wall torch to take it. Equip it and left-click to place on walls or ground. Torches light your way at night and can be moved freely.
Foxes
Skittish foxes roam the wilderness. They'll flee if you get too close -- try sneaking up on them.
☀ Day & Night
Time Cycle
Enable from the main menu. The sun arcs across the sky, torches ignite at dusk, and stars emerge at night. Snow biome has shorter days.
Fast-Forward
Hold Q to run the entire world at 3x speed -- time, movement, and animations all accelerate.
🎯 Camera & HUD
Camera Modes
Press V to switch between first-person and over-the-shoulder third-person view with a smooth cinematic transition.
Minimap
Top-right corner shows your position (yellow dot), buildings (brown), and flowers (cyan). Use it to navigate the world.
Hotbar
Press 1-5 to select inventory slots. Items auto-assign on pickup. Hold ALT to manage your inventory -- drag items between slots to reorder. Left-click uses the selected item.
